Araw ng Dabaw

📍 San Pedro Square

Celebrate the city's founding anniversary with a massive civic parade, the Mutya ng Dabaw pageant, and the Sayawan sa Da'n. It’s a city-wide birthday party where history meets modernity.

Duaw Davao

📍 Coastal Road & Rizal Park

Davao's official summer fest. Experience the "Duaw" (Visit) spirit with the Davao Pride Parade, Music Fests, and a showcase of the hottest lifestyle and tourism spots in the region.

Kadayawan Festival

📍 Downtown & Magsaysay Park

The "King of Festivals." A vibrant thanksgiving celebration honoring the 11 indigenous tribes, featuring the rhythmic Indak-Indak sa Kadalanan and the spectacular floral float parade.
